# 12-Month Lag Theory

Up: [[Arachne Intelligence]]

Finance's adoption of AI runs roughly **12 months behind software**. The lag is the opportunity: what's already normal in engineering (e.g., test-driven workflows) hasn't reached finance yet.

## Why it matters
- Anchors [[Test-Driven Building]] (importing a software discipline early).
- Sets the [[Finance Content]] narrator voice: a builder asking why finance resists what other domains adopted.
