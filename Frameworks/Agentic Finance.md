# Agentic Finance

Up: [[Arachne Intelligence]]

North-star vision: a finance department where AI agents handle routine operations (reporting, reconciliation, variance analysis, data prep) **autonomously**, freeing finance leaders for strategy and judgment. Truly agentic finance — beyond copilots and assistants.

## Why it matters
- Category-creation move: "the first person to credibly articulate autonomous finance operations becomes the node everyone connects to."
- The gap: every "AI for Finance" pitch talks about copilots; nobody articulates what actually-autonomous ops look like.

## Three-stage roadmap
copilot → supervised autonomy → new capabilities — with safety foregrounded throughout (see [[Active vs Passive AI Safety]]).
