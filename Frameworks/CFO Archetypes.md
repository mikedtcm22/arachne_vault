# CFO Archetypes

Up: [[Arachne Intelligence]]

A map of the finance-leadership market, identifying a gap: no mainstream archetype for a **Product-Driven Internal CFO**. My work aims to formalize archetype D.

## The four
- **(A) Internal CFO** — fundraising-first, investor-focused; output is board decks.
- **(B) Director, FP&A** — primary internal clarity, but seen as subordinate, not strategic.
- **(C) Fractional CFO** — hybrid; markets clarity but defaults to capital-readiness in delivery.
- **(D) Product-Driven Internal CFO** — my [[Finance-as-a-Service|FaaS]] vision: clarity-first system design, internal decision loops, real-time models, strategic operational leverage.
