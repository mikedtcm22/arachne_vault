# Active vs Passive AI Safety

Up: [[Arachne Intelligence]]

A distinction between safety enforced by **architecture** (active — the system structurally cannot do the unsafe thing) and safety that relies on **policy or behavior** (passive). [[Reclude]] is the concrete instance of the active layer.

## Why it matters
- Foregrounded in the [[Agentic Finance]] roadmap to de-risk moving from copilot toward autonomy.
