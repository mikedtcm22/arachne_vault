# Arachne — Overview

> Genesis note for the Arachne vault: what Arachne is, what it's for, and where its edges are. Living index — each pillar and system below should grow into its own linked note. Companion to [[Open Brain]].

## What Arachne is

Arachne is the work I do when I'm *not* "working" — everything I build outside the billable startup-finance practice. It's a deliberate **subset** of my life, and that subset is the whole source of its usefulness: the moment "Arachne" tries to mean everything, it stops meaning anything.

The name is about a stance — the craft is incidental. Arachne was the mortal weaver skilled enough to challenge a god, good enough to win or at worst tie, and punished anyway for the affront of being that good. The part worth keeping is defiance backed by real skill: the right to challenge authority that competence earns, and the injustice of being made small for it. She could as easily have been an archer or a singer. So thread-and-web imagery is welcome only where it happens to fit something I build — never as the organizing idea.

**Internally, the whole enterprise goes by [[Arachne Finance]]** — the umbrella, think personal holdco, under which each *Arachne instantiation* sits. An internal name rather than an external brand; publicly, the only place the Arachne name surfaces is the crypto / On-Chain work. Today there are two instantiations.

## What Arachne is for

Two pillars, split by purpose and (roughly) by calendar.

### [[Arachne Intelligence]] — Augmentation · weeknights

Building the systems, playbooks, frameworks, and bespoke tools that make me the best startup-finance professional I can be — as unfair an advantage in the finance marketplace as I can build.

The organizing principle is FaaS turned on myself: **the customer is "me, as a Startup Finance Professional."** This work feeds straight back into the day job — it's where I build the things I'll deploy there, and where I distill the insights that travel from one role to the next. The customer lens keeps it disciplined: every Augmentation project should make its customer measurably better at the work, and at the building.

The bet underneath it: software is moving toward everyone building bespoke tools for their own use-cases instead of buying off-the-shelf. I embrace that rather than fighting it with a SaaS company of my own — I become the AI-powered finance builder. That's why Reclude is a tool in my belt, not a business I run.

What lives here: the **frameworks** (FaaS, Agentic Finance, the Product-Driven Internal CFO archetype, the 12-month-lag read, TDB, the modeling disciplines), the **built tools** (Reclude, Open Brain, the Advisory Circle, the Comms Agent, the portfolio site), and the **positioning** that turns all of it into marketplace advantage — the career pitches (ResFrac, the Attivo VP-of-Finance move) and the public-facing finance content.

### [[Arachne On-Chain]] — weekends

The side-build pillar: in-wheelhouse projects outside the finance workflow. The container is simply "On-Chain," because on-chain is the whole of it right now — currently **[[EMBER]]**, payment-aware Bitcoin Ordinals that change state based on separate on-chain transactions. The door stays open for in-wheelhouse builds that aren't crypto, if one shows up.

## The boundary — what stays outside

Arachne earns its usefulness by being a subset, so the edges matter.

- **The billable practice is the vocation, and it lives outside Arachne.** Startup finance through Attivo — and a full-time finance seat if ResFrac converts — is the work itself. The line is the *activity*, not the subject matter: finance shows up on both sides, but *delivering the work* is "working," while *building the edge that makes the work better* is Arachne Intelligence. So the ResFrac role itself is the vocation, while the candidacy pitch and the reusable insights distilled from it are Arachne Intelligence. The Comms Agent processes consulting email and is still Arachne, because it's something I built to make myself better.
- **Ordinary life lives outside Arachne too** — the woodshop, family, Nashville, Tango. Good things, not Arachne things.

## Two memories: Open Brain + Arachne

This vault is the second of two memory systems, and the split is deliberate.

- **Open Brain — the thought database.** Atomic, additive, recombinant. Individual observations, ideas, and tasks captured fast and embedded for semantic recall, so they can collide and recombine later. Granular memory.
- **Arachne — the system wiki (this vault).** Integrated, structured, durable. Systems and playbooks that have congealed from many thoughts into a coherent structure I maintain and interlink. Structural memory.

The loop between them:
1. Thoughts accumulate in Open Brain and cluster around a theme.
2. When a cluster stabilizes into a real structure, it's **promoted** into an Arachne system note.
3. Working a system note throws off new questions and observations, **captured back** into Open Brain as fresh atomic thoughts.

→ [[Open Brain ↔ Arachne]] for the handoff rules.

## Conventions for this vault

- **Boundary rule:** if it's the billable work itself, or ordinary life, it doesn't get a note here.
